## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Introduction
- Basic concepts of biology.
- The Chemical Basis of Life
- Matter and chemical reactions.
- Matter and chemical reactions (continued).
- Water and life.
- Water and life (continued).
- Carbon skeletons and biological molecules.
- An overview of cell structure and function.
- The exchange of matter across cell membranes.
- Cellular metabolism processes: cellular respiration and fermentation.
- Cellular metabolism processes: cellular respiration and fermentation (continued).
- Cellular metabolism processes: photosynthesis.
- Structure of DNA, mechanisms of replication and DNA repair.
- Structure of chromosomes - Chromosome variations.
- Cell cycle, mitosis, and meiosis.
- Bacterial and viral genetics.

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Understand the fundamental and modern concepts of biology.
- G2.Explain the chemical basis of life.
- G3.Distinguish the structure and explain the function of cells, biological membranes, and organelles.
- G4.Understand the material basis and mechanisms of heredity and variation.
- G5.Apply techniques of modern genetic engineering.
- G6.Understand the concepts and mechanisms of speciation, evolution, and diversity in organisms.
