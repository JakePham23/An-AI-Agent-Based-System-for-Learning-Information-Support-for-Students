## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Describe the structure of atoms and molecules.
- G2.Explain the periodic variation of certain properties of chemical elements.
- G3.Recognize and distinguish the basic types of chemical bonds.
- G4.Identify and explain the relationship between fundamental interaction forces in matter and the physical properties of matter.
