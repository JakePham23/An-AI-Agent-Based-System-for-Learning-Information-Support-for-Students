# COURSE: Nhập môn mã hóa – mật mã (CSC15005)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: CSC15005
- **English Name**: Introduction to Cryptography
- **Vietnamese Name**: Nhập môn mã hóa – mật mã
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Assoc. Prof. Nguyễn Đình Thúc
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 5
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Introduction to cryptosystems
- . Symmetric cryptosystems.
- . Asymmetric cryptosystems.
- . Cryptographic hash functions.

- . Basic algorithms

- . RSA theorem
- . Proving the RSA theorem.
- RSA-cryptosystem: RSA implementation.
- . Big integers
- . Primes

- . Implementation
- . Security issues

- . Feistel network

- . Block and stream ciphers
- Applied crypt 1

- . Messenger Authentication Code - MAC
