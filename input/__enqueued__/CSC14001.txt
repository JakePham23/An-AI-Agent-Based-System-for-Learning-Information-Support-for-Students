# COURSE: Automata và ngôn ngữ hình thức (CSC14001)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: CSC14001
- **English Name**: Automata and Formal Languages
- **Vietnamese Name**: Automata và ngôn ngữ hình thức
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Dr. Nguyễn Thanh Phương
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 6
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Introduction to the basic mathematics for automata theory and formal languages
- Introduction to automata and formal languages
- Finite automata
- Regular expressions
- Regular grammars and regular languages
- Context-free grammars and context-free languages
- Push-down automata
- Turing machines and Church-Turing thesis
- The Chomsky Hierarchy and Beyond

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Analyze and propose reasonable solutions to problems in computer science.
- G2.Interpret English terminology in the theory of computer science.
- G3.Explain the nature, role, and operation of computational structures and their variants.
- G4.Analyze the properties of classes of formal languages.
- G5.Construct automata recognizing each class of formal languages.
- G6.Construct formal grammars generating each class of formal languages.
