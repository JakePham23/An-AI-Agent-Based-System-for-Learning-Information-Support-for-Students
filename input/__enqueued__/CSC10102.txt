- G1.Understand the practical working environment and culture of some current IT companies.
- G2.Apply and coordinate specialized skills, soft skills to grasp and understand issues related to the profession.
- G3.Analyzing and evaluating one's own career orientation.
- G4.Students will learn additional soft skills required for the workplace and update their knowledge with new technologies.
- G5.Understand the issues related to professional responsibility and ethics.
