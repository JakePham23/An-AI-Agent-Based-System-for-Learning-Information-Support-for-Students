# COURSE: Kinh tế chính trị Mác - Lênin (BAA00102)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: BAA00102
- **English Name**: Marxist-Leninist Political Economics
- **Vietnamese Name**: Kinh tế chính trị Mác - Lênin
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Ngô Tuấn Phương
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 5
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 25 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 0 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 57.5 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Introduction to the Subject
- Chapter 1: Subject, Research Methods, and Functions of Marxist-Leninist Political Economy
- Chapter 2: Commodities, Markets, and the Roles of Market Participants
- Chapter 3: Surplus Value in a Market Economy
- Chapter 4: Competition and Monopoly in a Market Economy
- Chapter 5: Socialist-Oriented Market Economy and Economic Interest Relations in Vietnam
- Chapter 6: Industrialization, Modernization, and Vietnam's Integration into the Global Economy

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G2.Summarize the system of viewpoints and economic laws. Evaluate and recognize the nature of economic interests in the market economy.
- G4.Apply the knowledge they have learned as a foundation for understanding the basic principles of Marxist-Leninist philosophy, and encourage them to fight against erroneous arguments that deny the formation and development of Marxist-Leninist philosophy."
- G1.Understand the system of viewpoints and the theoretical framework of Marxist-Leninist political economy. Establish the most fundamental theoretical basis to approach the content of the Ho Chi Minh Thought course and the Revolutionary Path of the Communist Party of Vietnam.
- G3.Address practical issues that arise. Distinguish right from wrong and engage in critical thinking. Choose practical activities based on the methodology of Marxism-Leninism.
