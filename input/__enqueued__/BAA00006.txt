# COURSE: Tâm lý đại cương (BAA00006)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: BAA00006
- **English Name**: Introduction to Psychology
- **Vietnamese Name**: Tâm lý đại cương
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Kim Thị Dung
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 4
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 25 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 0 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 57.5 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Issue 1: General Overview of Psychology
- Issue 2: Subject, Tasks, and Research Methods
- Issue 3: Natural and Social Foundations of the Formation of Psychology and Consciousness
- Issue 4: Sensory Cognitive Processes
- Issue 5: Rational Cognitive Processes
- Issue 6: Memory and Attention
- Issue 7: Emotions and Feelings
- Issue 8: Willpower
- Issue 9: Personality

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G3.Understand the role and functions of psychological phenomena.
- G1.Understand the nature, mechanisms of emergence, and formation of human psychological phenomena."
- G2.Identify and explain the characteristic features, specific manifestations, and the relationships between basic psychological phenomena of human beings."
