## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Equip students with knowledge about the relationship between humans and nature, including non-living factors and other organisms surrounding humans
- G2.Knowledge: Understand the position, duties, and roles of humans within the ecosystem
- G3.Skills: Recognize the outcomes of human activities and their impacts on natural and social life
- G4.Attitude: diligence and commitment
- G5.Demonstrate soft skills (teamwork, presentation)
