## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Use simple equipment in the chemistry lab to measure volume and determine density.
- G2.Use the titration method to determine the concentration of acetic acid.
- G3.Identify and distinguish the properties of acids and bases, as well as their effects.
- G4.Identify the factors affecting the reaction rate.
- G5.Determine the amount of phosphorus in a chemical compound while also identifying the components of the precipitate.
- G6.Synthesize alum from aluminum cans.
- G7.Identify and distinguish important functional groups in organic compounds.
- G8.Synthesize aspirin from salicylic acid.
- G9.Prepare soap and detergents.
