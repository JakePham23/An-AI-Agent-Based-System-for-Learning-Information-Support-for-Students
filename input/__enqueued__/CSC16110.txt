# COURSE: Chuyên đề Đồ họa máy tính (CSC16110)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: CSC16110
- **English Name**: Selected Topics in Computer Graphics
- **Vietnamese Name**: Chuyên đề Đồ họa máy tính
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Phạm Thanh Tùng
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 7
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Introduction to 3D Computer Graphics
- The basic concept of 3D graphic modeling space
- Definitions and methods of simulating colors and materials
- Three-dimensional transformation method
- Illumination simulation method
- Projection and viewport
- Material surface simulation method
- Reflection simulation method
- Shadow simulation method
- Motion simulation method
- Object interaction in 3D space
- WebGL & Three.js
- Seminar on simulation project of objects in 3D space
- Seminar on 3D game project

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Practice on an individual and groups to present a number of technical reports in the field of digital computer graphics
- G2.Interpret object simulation methods in 3D space
- G3.Interpret the motion and interaction simulation methods in 3D space
- G4.Practice  computer programming using WebGL toolkit
- G5.Recognize 3D graphics in accademic and industry context
