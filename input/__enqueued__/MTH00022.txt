## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Master the knowledge of differential calculus for multivariable functions.
- G2.Master the knowledge of multiple integrals.
- G3.Master the knowledge of line integrals and provide a brief introduction to surface integrals.
- G4.Master the knowledge of differential equations.
- G5.Apply knowledge to solve real-world application problems and other subjects, such as finding extrema of multivariable functions, area of plane figures, volume of solids, work done by vector fields, calculating arc length, and surface area; solve basic problems involving differential equations.
- G6.Know how to work in a team
