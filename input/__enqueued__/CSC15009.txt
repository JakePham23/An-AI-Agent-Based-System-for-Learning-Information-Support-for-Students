# COURSE: Xử lý tín hiệu số (CSC15009)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: CSC15009
- **English Name**: Digital Signal Processing
- **Vietnamese Name**: Xử lý tín hiệu số
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Thái Hùng Văn
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 7
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Overview of signals & digital signal processing.
- Digital signal sampling, playback and some basic processing
- Discrete signal processing systems and linear invariant response
- Basic transform on discrete signals - Z transform
- Fourier, Waveless, LPC transforms
- Review - Relationship between processes and transformations.
- Noise Filter
- Embedding & hiding data in digital signals
- Compress & decompress digital signals
- Digital voice and audio signal processing
- Review

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Understand the specialized English term and materials related to data recovery and safety (DSR)
- G2.Understand the basic knowledge and theory in Digital Signal
Processing
- G3.Understand basic concepts, properties of discrete time systems, analyze & calculate impulse response and response of invariant linear system
- G4.Understand and apply the fundamental transformations Z, Fourier
- G5.Understand and apply some methods of noise filtering, embedding or hiding data & signal compression
- G6.Understand and apply some basic processing with digital audio signals.
