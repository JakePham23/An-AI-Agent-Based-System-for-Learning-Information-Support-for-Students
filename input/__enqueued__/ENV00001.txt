## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Practice teamwork skills
- G2.Present the basic concepts of the environment and resources
- G3.Present the environmental issues in the world and Vietnam
- G4.Present solutions for environmental protection and resource conservation
- G5.Develop a positive attitude towards the interactive relationship between humans and the environment
