# COURSE: Vật lý đại cương 1 (Cơ - Nhiệt) (PHY00001)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: PHY00001
- **English Name**: General Physics 1 (Mechanics - Thermodynamics)
- **Vietnamese Name**: Vật lý đại cương 1 (Cơ - Nhiệt)
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Assoc. Prof. Huỳnh Trúc Phương
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 2
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 0 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 86.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Physics and measurements
- Kinematics of particles
- Dynamics of particles
- Conservation laws in mechanics
- Mechanics of solids
- Ideal gases
- First law of thermodynamics
- Second law of thermodynamics

- G4.Apply the laws and principles to solve problems related to mechanics and thermodynamics
