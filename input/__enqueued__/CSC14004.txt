## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Show ability to work at an individual and team level to present a sample of scientific reports and present subject-related content.
- G2.Explain English terms in the field of data mining.
- G3.Explain basic concepts and terminology in the field of data mining.
- G4.Express the meaning and application of data mining problems in real-world problems.
- G5.Explain how basic algorithms work in data mining.
- G6.Conduct some components of a small-scale data mining system.
- G7.Use software tools.
