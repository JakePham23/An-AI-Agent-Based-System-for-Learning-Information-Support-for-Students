# COURSE: Kinh tế đại cương (BAA00005)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: BAA00005
- **English Name**: Introduction to Economics
- **Vietnamese Name**: Kinh tế đại cương
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Dr. Nguyễn Thị Đông
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 4
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 25 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 0 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 57.5 hours

- Government Intervention Failures

- Budget Constraints
- Consumer Choice

- Production and Production Costs
- Perfectly Competitive Markets
- Monopoly Markets

- Methods for Calculating National Output Indicators
- Price Issues in Calculating National Output Indicators
- Aggregate Supply, Aggregate Demand, and Equilibrium Output

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Apply economic concepts, principles, and tools to analyze economic issues.
- G2.Understand the nature of various issues and phenomena discussed in the media, both micro and macro, such as national accounting indicators like GDP, GNP, and certain economic indexes.
- G3.Analyze price fluctuations in both domestic and international markets. The course will also foster independent thinking through individual assignments and in-class discussions.
- G4.Apply economic knowledge to analyze, synthesize, and evaluate how government economic policies might affect consumers and markets.
