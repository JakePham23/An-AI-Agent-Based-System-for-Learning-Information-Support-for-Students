# COURSE: Pháp luật đại cương (BAA00004)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: BAA00004
- **English Name**: Introduction to Law
- **Vietnamese Name**: Pháp luật đại cương
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Hoàng Thanh Tú
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 2
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 0 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 86.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Chapter 1: General Introduction to the State and Law
- Lesson 1. The Origin, Nature, Characteristics, and Functions of the State.
- Lesson 2. The Origin, Nature, Characteristics, and Role of Law
- Chapter2: The State Apparatus.
- Lesson 3. Type of State, Form of State.
- Lesson 4. The State Apparatus of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam.
- Chapter 3: Law – A Tool for Regulating Legal Relations.
- Lesson 5. Legal Norms, Legal Relations, and the System of Legal Normative Documents in Vietnam.
- Lesson 6. Implementation of the Law, Violation of the Law, and Legal Awareness
- Chapter 4: Branches of Law in the Legal System of Vietnam
- Lesson 7: Constitutional Law
- Lesson 8: Civil Law
- Lesson 9: Civil Procedure Law
- Lesson 10: Criminal Law
- Lesson 11: Civil Procedure Law
- Chapter 5: General issues of anti-corruption
- Lesson 12. Concept and classification of corrupt behaviors
- Lesson 13. Dealing with corrupt behaviors, responsibilities of individuals, agencies, and organizations in anti-corruption efforts
- Review
