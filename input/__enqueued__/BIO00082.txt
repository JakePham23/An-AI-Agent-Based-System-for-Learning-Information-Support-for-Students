## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Distinguish between the tissues and organs of monocotyledonous (monocots) and dicotyledonous (dicots) plants
- G2.Understand the qualitative principles of certain protective plant pigments and photosynthetic pigments
- G3.Demonstrate the effects of environmental conditions on photosynthesis in plants
- G4.Demonstrate the manifestation of respiration, fermentation in plants, and the presence of enzymes in these processes
- G5.Demonstrate the effects of environmental conditions on water transport in vascular tissue
- G6.Understand and identify the different types of animal tissues
- G7.Understand and demonstrate the existence of bioelectricity in the body
- G8.Distinguish between the types of vessels in the mesenteric vascular system of frogs; explain the effects of hormones on the blood flow rate in the vessels
- G9.Understand the influence of climatic factors on the distribution of certain types of ecosystems
