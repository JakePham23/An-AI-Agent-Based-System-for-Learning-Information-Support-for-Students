## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G6.Apply the methodological principles derived from the theoretical content of historical materialism to analyze the understanding and creative application of the Communist Party of Vietnam in the practical context of the Vietnamese revolution.
- G1.Summarize the inevitable process of the formation of the Communist Party of Vietnam and the Party's first political platform.
- G2.Describe the Party's leadership process through different periods: from 1930 to 1945; from 1945 to 1975; and from 1975 to the present.
- G3.Generalize and interpret the development of the Party's policies and leadership during the period when the whole country transitioned to socialism and implemented the renovation process from after national reunification in 1975 to the present.
- G4.Identify the Party's thinking on industrialization to demonstrate issues or events related to the industrialization of the country.
- G5.Identify the Party's thinking on the market economy to demonstrate issues or events related to the country's economy.
- G6.Identify the Party's thinking on culture and society to demonstrate issues or events related to the country.
- G7.Identify the Party's thinking on foreign affairs to demonstrate issues or events related to the country's foreign relations.
- G8.Share a proper understanding of the Party's leadership in the Vietnamese revolution.
- G9.Respect training regulations, and actively participate fully and dynamically in contributing creatively to lessons.
- G10.Analyze and evaluate the achievements, limitations, and leadership experiences of the Party in the renovation process.
- G11.Analyze and evaluate the great victories of the Vietnamese revolution and the major lessons about the Party's leadership.
- G12.Apply the knowledge learned into practical work.
