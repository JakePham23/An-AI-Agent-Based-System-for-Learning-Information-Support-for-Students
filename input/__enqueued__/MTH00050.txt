## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Explain the concepts of graphs and apply them to solve basic graph problems.
- G2.Apply the learned algorithms to solve various tree-related problems.
- G3.Explain and solve problems related to path finding on graphs.
- G4.Use combinatorial theory to solve counting problems.
- G5.Construct generating functions and use them to solve counting problems.
- G6.Apply the principle of inclusion-exclusion and the polynomial of rooks to solve counting problems.
- G7.Explain the definitions of some advanced counting principles.
