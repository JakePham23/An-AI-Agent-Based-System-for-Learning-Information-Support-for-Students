# COURSE: Hệ thống thông tin doanh nghiệp (CSC12109)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: CSC12109
- **English Name**: Introduction to Enterprise Information Systems
- **Vietnamese Name**: Hệ thống thông tin doanh nghiệp
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Assoc. Prof. Lê Nguyễn Hoài Nam
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 5
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

- Business applications

- EC,
