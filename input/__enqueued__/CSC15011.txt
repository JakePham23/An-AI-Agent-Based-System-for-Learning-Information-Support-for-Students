# COURSE: Nhập môn ngôn ngữ học thống kê và ứng dụng (CSC15011)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: CSC15011
- **English Name**: Introduction to Statistics Linguistics and Applications
- **Vietnamese Name**: Nhập môn ngôn ngữ học thống kê và ứng dụng
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Assoc. Prof. Đinh Điền - Nguyễn Hồng Bửu Long
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 7
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- An overview of statistical linguistics:
- Probability theory
- Random variables, probability distribution
- Statistical linguistics
- Parameter estimation and statistical hypothesis testing
- Correlation and regression
- Frequency dictionary
- Quantitative learning style
- Quantitative organization of vocabulary
- Diversity of vocabulary
- Introduction to linguistics:
- Introduction
- History of corpus research
- Linguistic theory
- Building a corpus:
- Planning to build a corpus
- Criteria for selecting a corpus
- Types of a corpus
- Building a corpus (continued):
- Collect data
- Building a monolingual corpus (Vietnamese)
- Building a bilingual corpus (English-Vietnamese)
- Processing a corpus:
- Pre-processing of a corpus
- Language labeling
- Formalization of language labels
- Processing the corpus (continued):
- Language labeling for a monolingual corpus (Vietnamese)
- Language labeling for a bilingual corpus (English-Vietnamese)
- Designing a bilingual corpus (English-Vietnamese)
- Processing a corpus (continued):
- Corpus marking
- Metadata
- Spoken text corpus
- Applications:
- In computational linguistics
- In language research
- In language teaching
- In other language-related applications
- Seminar

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Apply team work and individual work skills to research, construct, and exploit corpora
- G2.Understand and apply at a basic level statistical methods, evaluating on real data
- G3.Know the basic concepts of corpus, classification of corpus; criteria for building corpus, processing and exploiting corpus
- G4.Identify, describe, analyze, and evaluate the structure and quality of a particular corpus
- G6.Recognize the role and importance of statistical linguistics in preparing corpora
- G5.Know and apply the criteria when collecting corpus (corpus balancing, representativeness, corpus sampling); corpus processing: language labeling at different linguistic levels and at different language levels; corpus mining
