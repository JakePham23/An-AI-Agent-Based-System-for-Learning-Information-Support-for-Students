## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Improve communication skills: The course aims to enhance learners' ability to communicate in everyday and professional situations, including both speaking and writing.
- G2.Expand vocabulary and grammar: It helps students master more complex grammatical structures and expand their vocabulary on a variety of topics.
- G3.Develop listening and reading comprehension: The course strengthens the ability to understand spoken and written texts of intermediate difficulty, including conversations at a natural speed.
- G4.Enhance writing skills: Students will practice writing clear, coherent, and context-appropriate texts (e.g., emails, short reports, brief essays).
- G5.Prepare for English proficiency tests: For university programs, this course may help learners prepare for standardized exams like TOEIC, IELTS, or local English assessments.
