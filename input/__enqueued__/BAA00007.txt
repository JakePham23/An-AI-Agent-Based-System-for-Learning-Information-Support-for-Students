# COURSE: Phương pháp luận sáng tạo (BAA00007)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: BAA00007
- **English Name**: Creativity Methodologies
- **Vietnamese Name**: Phương pháp luận sáng tạo
- **Responsible Person**: for the module:
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 4

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Chapter 1: Introduction (2 periods)
- Some basic concepts.
- Subjects, objectives, benefits, and significance of the course on creative methodology..
- The science of creativity and creative methodology: a brief history from Heuristics to Creatology.
- Chapter 2: Natural Methods for Problem Solving and Decision Making – Overview of Approaches to Building Creative Methodology – TRIZ (2 periods).
- Trial and error method.
- Advantages and disadvantages of the trial and error method.
- Classification of levels of creativity and levels of difficulty of problems.
- Overview of approaches in the field of creative methodology.
- Theory of solving inventive problems (TRIZ): basic ideas and sources of knowledge.
- Block diagram of the theory of solving inventive problems (TRIZ).
- Chapter 3: Some scientific and technical knowledge as the foundation of the subject (12 periods)
- From needs to actions and vice versa.
- Model of transforming information into knowledge in the process of problem-solving and decision-making.
- Psychological factors and processes in creative thinking.
- Psychological inertia.
- Materialist dialectics and dialectical thinking.
- Types of contradictions in problem-solving and decision-making.
- Systems and systems thinking.
- System inertia.
- Regarding the two concepts: invention and innovation. The role and benefits of patent information in building creative methodology.
- Chapter 4: Basic creative tricks (principles) (12 periods)
- Things to note about basic creative tricks (principles).
- Text presenting the basic creative principles.
- Program for discovering tricks and reconstructing the thinking process to achieve the intended objects.
- Program for streamlining the problem-solving and decision-making process.
- Table of principles used to solve technical contradictions.
- System of model transformations used to solve physical contradictions.
- From principles to methods.
- Chapter 5: Methods for enhancing creative thinking (6 periods)
- Method of Focal Objects.
- Morphological Analysis method.
- Check-listing Method.
- Brainstorming method.
- Synectics method (using analogies).
- Chapter 6: Laws of system development (10 periods)
- Laws of system development.
- The lifecycle of a system.
- Diagram of the potential developments of a system.
- Creative principles and methods: specific representations of the laws of system development.
- Chapter 7: Summary of the introductory program on creative methodology (1 period)
- Reasons for economic success in the 19th and 20th centuries.
- Reasons for economic success in the 21st century.
- Challenges.
- Consequences of the challenges.
- Requirements and actions needed to cope with the challenges.
- New skills for the future.

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Provide a system of ways to consider phenomena.
- G2.Enhance observation skills and creative curiosity.
- G3.Analyze and logically explain existing creative solutions.
- G4.Increase sensitivity in acquiring and evaluating the value of information.
- G5.Recognize the unified similarities among systems that seem very different.
- G6.Overcome psychological inertia.
- G7.Help identify available reserves in the system, especially free reserves that can be utilized.
- G8.Propose and select appropriate approaches to solve problems.
- G9.Resolve conflicts present in the problem.
- G10.Generate ideas for improving the given system.
- G11.Forecast the developmental trends of the given system in the future.
- G12.Assist in identifying, formulating, and selecting the problems that need to be solved.
- G13.Practice developing creative imagination.
- G14.Improve oneself, establish a systematic, creative work style and mindset.
- G15.Contribute to building systematic and dialectical thinking.
