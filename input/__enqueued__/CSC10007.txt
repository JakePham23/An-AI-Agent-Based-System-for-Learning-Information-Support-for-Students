- **English Name**: Operating Systems

- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Explain a lot of practical information about how programming languages, operating systems, and architectures interact and how to use each effectively.
- G2.Show how the operating system is structured and its components work
- G3.Use and manipulate computers and computer program
- G4.Use the specialized English terminology in Operating System
- G5.Apply some skills at the individual and the teamwork level to present problems in the Computer Networking
