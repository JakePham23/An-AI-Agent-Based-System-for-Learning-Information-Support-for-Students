# COURSE: Cơ sở trí tuệ nhân tạo (CSC14003)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: CSC14003
- **English Name**: Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence
- **Vietnamese Name**: Cơ sở trí tuệ nhân tạo
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Dr. Nguyễn Ngọc Thảo
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 5
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Introduction to AI
- Intelligent Agents
- Solving Problem by Searching: Basic concepts and Uninformed search
- Solving Problem by Searching: Informed search
- Local Search
- Adversarial Search
- Constraint Satisfaction Problem
- Midterm Examination
- Propositional Logic: Basic concepts
- Propositional Logic: Inference Mechanisms
- First-Order Logic: Syntax, Semantics, and Inference Mechanisms
- Machine learning: Basic concepts and ID3 Decision tree
- Perceptron and Multi-layer neural networks
- Reviews

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Formulate fundamental AI problems using appropriate AI concepts and terminologies.
- G2.Develop search agents to solve common problem-solving tasks.
- G3.Develop logical agents whose knowledge is represented using either propositional logic or first-order logic.
- G4.Develop simple learning agents that learn by observations.
- G5.Practice generating hypotheses and assess them through theoretical and/or empirical evaluations.
- G6.Recognize textbook, assignments and practice writing reports in English.
- G7.Organize tasks while conducting individual and group work.
