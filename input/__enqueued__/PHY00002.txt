# COURSE: Vật lý đại cương 2 (Điện từ - Quang) (PHY00002)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: PHY00002
- **English Name**: General Physics 2 (Electromagnetic - Optics)
- **Vietnamese Name**: Vật lý đại cương 2 (Điện từ - Quang)
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Assoc. Prof. Huỳnh Trúc Phương
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 3
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 0 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 86.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Electrostatic field in a vacuum
- Conductor
- Steady current
- Magnetic field in a vacuum
- Electromagnetic induction
- Foundations of light optics
- Light interference
- Light diffraction
- Light polarization

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Work at both individual and group collaboration levels to present technical reports according to a provided template on electromagnetism and light optics problems
- G2.Understand English terminology related to the subject
- G3.Explain the basic concepts of electric fields, magnetic fields, and light optics
- G4.Apply the laws and principles to solve problems related to electromagnetism and optics
- G5.Understand the principles of ethics and responsibility in learning
