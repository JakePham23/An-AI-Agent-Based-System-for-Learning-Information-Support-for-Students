# COURSE: Nhập môn thiết kế và phân tích giải thuật (CSC14111)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: CSC14111
- **English Name**: Introduction to Design and Analysis of Algorithms
- **Vietnamese Name**: Nhập môn thiết kế và phân tích giải thuật
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Dr. Nguyễn Thanh Phương
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 5
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- General introduction to design and analysis of algorithms
- Fundamentals of the analysis of algorithm efficiency
- Brute-force design technique
- Backtracking design technique
- Divide-and-conquer design technique
- Decrease-and-conquer design technique
- Dynamic programming
- Greedy design technique

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Propose solutions to problems by thinking independently.
- G2.Interpret English terminology in the field of algorithm design and analysis.
- G3.Evaluate the complexity of algorithms by applying methods.
- G4.Compare and apply major algorithm design techniques by analyzing them.
- G5.Solve classes of problems commonly encountered in computer science by analyzing them.
• Content:
- General introduction to design and analysis of algorithms
- Fundamentals of the analysis of algorithm efficiency
- Brute-force design technique
- Backtracking design technique
- Divide-and-conquer design technique
- Decrease-and-conquer design technique
- Dynamic programming
- Greedy design technique
