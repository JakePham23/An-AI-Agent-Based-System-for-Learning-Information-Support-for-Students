## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Enhance communication skills in English: Develop advanced listening, speaking, reading, and writing skills. Students will improve their ability to communicate in academic and professional situations.
- G2.Improve academic writing skills: Focus on writing essays, reports, or other academic texts clearly and with proper structure. Students will learn to argue, analyze, and present their viewpoints logically.
- G3.Develop presentation skills: Train students to present ideas and give public presentations in English confidently and professionally.
- G4.Expand vocabulary and grammar knowledge: Improve specialized, technical, and academic vocabulary while reinforcing grammar knowledge for accurate application in real-life communication situations.
- G5.Enhance listening comprehension and reflexes: Develop the ability to understand complex lectures, conversations, and listening materials. Students will become familiar with various English accents and learn to respond quickly.
- G6.Prepare for international English exams: In some programs, English 4 may aim to help students prepare for English proficiency exams such as TOEFL, IELTS, or similar tests, which require a higher level of English proficiency.
- G7.Develop teamwork skills: Students may participate in group projects, discussions, and collaboration with classmates to complete academic tasks, thereby improving their teamwork skills in English.
