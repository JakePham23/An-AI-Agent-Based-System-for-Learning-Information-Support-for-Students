# COURSE: Hệ thống thông tin phục vụ trí tuệ kinh doanh (CSC12107)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: CSC12107
- **English Name**: Information Systems for Business Intelligence
- **Vietnamese Name**: Hệ thống thông tin phục vụ trí tuệ kinh doanh
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Hồ Thị Hoàng Vy
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 7
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Chapter 1: overview of business intelligence
- * Describe the business intelligence (B I) concepts and relate them to DSS
- * Summarize main applications and business value for BI
- * Understand the various types of analytics
- * Information gathering
- * Decision-making
- Chapter 2: Data warehouse
- * What’s a data warehouse
- * DW architecture
- * DW development
- Chapter 3: data modeling
- * Dimensional data store
- * Dimension/fact table
- * Data hierarchy
- Slowly changing dimension
- Chapter 3: data modeling
- Chapter 4: ETL process
- * Introduction to ETL
- * ETL approach and architecture
- * Extracting data sources
- Chapter 4: ETL process
- Populate the DW (stage loading, NDS, dimension/fact table, refresh data)
- Chapter 5: Business intelligence OLAP
- Chapter 6: Business intelligence report, dashboard
- Chapter 7: Business intelligence mining
- Chapter 7: Business intelligence mining
- Review

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Apply teamwork skills to present or discuss related topics/concepts about DW and BI
- G2.Use the skill of reading technical documents about data warehouse in English
- G3.Analyze business requirements and applying the basic knowledge for the data warehouse design phases
- G4.Distinguish the main data mining tasks and some algorithms for predictive and descriptive analysis on business intelligence.
- G5.Conduct a DW/BI project with MS BI tool
