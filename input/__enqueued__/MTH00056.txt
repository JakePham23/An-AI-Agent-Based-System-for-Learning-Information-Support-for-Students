# COURSE: Phương pháp toán cho trí tuệ nhân tạo (MTH00056)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: MTH00056
- **English Name**: Mathematics for Artificial Intelligence
- **Vietnamese Name**: Phương pháp toán cho trí tuệ nhân tạo
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Assoc. Prof. Nguyễn Đình Thúc
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 4
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
Specifically, the course focuses on three basic models: optimal models, dynamical models, and probabilistic models.
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Introduction to discrete structures:
- combinatorics and
- graph structures
- Graph search algorithms:
- Depth First Search
- Breadth First Search (BFT)
- Connected Graphs
- Connectivity in graphs
- Complete Graphs
- Strongly Connected Components
- Some applied math problems of graphs
- Function optimal models
- Optimizing a 1-variable function
- Optimizing multivariable function
- Calculation method for optimization
- Dynamic models
- Introduction to dynamic models
- Analysis of dynamic models
- Simulation of dynamic models
- Some applied math problems of dynamic modeling
- Probabilistic models
- Introduction to probabilistic models
- Random samples
- Simulate probabilistic models
- Some applied math problems of probability modeling
- Review
