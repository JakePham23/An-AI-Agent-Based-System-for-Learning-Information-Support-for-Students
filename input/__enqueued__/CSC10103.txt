- **English Name**: Entrepreneurship in IT

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Establish a working group at an appropriate scale and assign tasks and responsibilities to team members.
- G2.Explain basic concepts and terminology in both Vietnamese and English related to the field of Entrepreneurship.
- G3.Write technical reports according to provided templates and present the results in front of audience.
- G4.Understand the steps involved in the process of building an IT startup.
- G5.Generate ideas, develop plans, and execute the implementation of a product at a prototype level.
• Content:
