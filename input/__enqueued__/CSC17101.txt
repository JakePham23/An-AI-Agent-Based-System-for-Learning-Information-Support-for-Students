# COURSE: Hệ thống tư vấn (CSC17101)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: CSC17101
- **English Name**: Recommender Systems
- **Vietnamese Name**: Hệ thống tư vấn
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Assoc. Prof. Lê Nguyễn Hoài Nam
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 6
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 25 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 116.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- An Introduction to Recommender Systems:
- -Goals of Recommender Systems
- -Recommendation Applications
- -Basic concepts
- Neighborhood-Based Collaborative Filtering:
- -Introduction
- -User-Based Neighborhood Models
- -Item-Based Neighborhood Models
- -Similarity measure
- -Efficient
- -Implementation and Computational Complexity
- Model-Based Collaborative Filtering:
- -Introduction
- -Decision and Regression Trees
- -Rule-Based Collaborative Filtering
- -Naive Bayes Collaborative Filtering
- Model-Based Collaborative Filtering:
- -Latent Factor Models
- -Strengths and Weaknesses
- -Efficient
- -Implementation and Computational Complexity
- Content-Based Recommender Systems:
- -Introduction
- -Preprocessing and Feature Extraction
- -Content-Based Versus Collaborative Recommendations
- Hybrid Recommender Systems:
- -Introduction
- -Weighted Hybrids
- -Switching Hybrids
- -Feature Augmentation Hybrids
- -Meta-Level Hybrids
- Feature Combination Hybrids
- -Mixed Hybrids.
- Evaluating Recommender Systems:
- -Online Evaluation
- -Offline Evaluation
- -Metrics
- Group recommender system:
- -Introduction
- -Profile aggregation
- -Recommendation aggregation
- -Evaluation
- Integrating implicit feedback into recommender systems
- Integrating textual reviews into recommender systems
- Review
