## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Develop advanced communication skills: Learners will be able to communicate more confidently and fluently in complex situations, including academic and professional discussions.
- G2.Enhance the use of advanced grammar and specialized vocabulary: Learners will expand their academic and specialized vocabulary and master the use of advanced grammatical structures.
- G3.Improve listening comprehension in complex environments: The course helps learners understand and analyze complex conversations or speeches, such as academic lectures or professional discussions.
- G4.Develop advanced reading comprehension skills: Students will practice understanding academic texts, research papers, or specialized reports with a high level of difficulty.
- G5.Enhance academic and professional writing skills: The course will help students write academic essays, research reports, or detailed and coherent specialized documents.
- G6.Prepare for real-world work situations: Learners will be equipped with skills to communicate, discuss, and work in English in international or academic environments.
- G7.Practice critical thinking and argumentation: Students will develop their ability to present and defend their opinions in discussions and debates in English
