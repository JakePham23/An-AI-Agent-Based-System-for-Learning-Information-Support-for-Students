## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Develop basic communication skills: Help students master basic sentence patterns for communication in everyday situations such as self-introduction, asking for directions, shopping, or discussing common topics.
- G2.Improve listening and speaking skills: Enhance the ability to understand spoken English and respond in conversations. Students will practice through listening exercises and dialogues with the teacher or peers.
- G3.Reinforce basic grammar and vocabulary: Learn essential grammar structures such as the present, past, and future tenses, conditional sentences, and vocabulary related to everyday communication topics.
- G4.Develop reading comprehension skills: Improve the ability to understand simple texts, including grasping the main idea and details of paragraphs.
- G5.Practice basic writing skills: Students will learn how to write short paragraphs, emails, or simple essays on familiar topics.
- G6.Boost confidence in using English: Foster confidence when using English, helping students feel more comfortable expressing themselves in the language.
