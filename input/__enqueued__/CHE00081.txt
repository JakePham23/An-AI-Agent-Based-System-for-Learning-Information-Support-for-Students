## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Use Excel software for calculations, graphing, and presenting results.
- G2.Use simple equipment in the chemistry lab for measuring, titrating, and purifying.
- G3.Use volumetric and mass measuring instruments to titrate acetic acid in vinegar.
- G4.Identify the reactants used in excess or deficiency.
- G5.Determine the acidity or basicity of a solution by measuring its pH. Explain the causes of this pH and its physical and chemical effects.
- G6.Investigate the factors affecting chemical equilibrium in solutions of acid-base reactions, precipitation reactions, and complex formation reactions.
- G7.Identify the thermodynamic parameters of the borax dissolution process.
- G8.Determine the order and activation energy of a chemical reaction.
- G9.Determine the electrode potential from the electrochemical cell system.
