## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Understanding fundamental concepts such as integers, prime numbers, least common multiples (LCM), greatest common divisors (GCD), and related theorems
- G2.Enhance the ability to analyze and solve problems through number-related exercises
- G3.Introduce students to proof techniques, including proof by contradiction, mathematical induction, and the use of theorems
- G4.Study the applications of number theory in cryptography, computer science, and other areas of science and engineering
- G5.Explore and find creative solutions to complex mathematical problems
