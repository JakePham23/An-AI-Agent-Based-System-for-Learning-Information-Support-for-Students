## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Understand and explain the thermal effects accompanying chemical and physical changes.
- G2.Understand and explain the conditions for reactions to occur naturally and for chemical equilibrium to be established.
- G3.Understand and explain chemical equilibrium and the factors affecting it.
- G4.Understand and explain reaction rates and the factors influencing these rates.
- G5.Understand and explain the types of equilibria in solutions: acid-base, precipitation, and complex formation.
- G6.Understand and explain electrochemical cells, predict the direction of redox reactions, and natural corrosion processes.
- G7.Identify the correlation between the factors affecting properties and transformations in the reaction system.
