## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Apply teamwork skills to investigate a specific problem in data science.
- G2.Explain the English terms used in the field of data science.
- G3.Explain methods for collecting data from the web.
- G4.Describe the statistical values of centrality and distribution of each component, sampling method, and research design.
- G5.Construct a suitable approach to visualize and preprocess data for a given problem.
- G6.Apply machine learning models and analyze the archived results.
- G7.Summarize the whole process of data science and the methods to communicate results.
