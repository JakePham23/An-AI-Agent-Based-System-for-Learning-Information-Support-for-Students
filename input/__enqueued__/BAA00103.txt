# COURSE: Chủ nghĩa xã hội khoa học (BAA00103)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: BAA00103
- **English Name**: Scientific Socialism
- **Vietnamese Name**: Chủ nghĩa xã hội khoa học
- **Responsible Person**: for the module: Dr. Nguyễn Đình Quốc Cường
- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 6
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 25 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 0 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 57.5 hours

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Understand and systematically analyze the basic contents of the scientific socialism course.
- G3.Understand the scientific grounds for combating distorted perceptions and the sabotage by hostile forces.
- G4.Apply the knowledge of scientific socialism to evaluate and solve political and social issues arising in practice.
- G5.Apply the methodological principles derived from the theoretical content of dialectical materialism to cognition and practice
- G2.Analyze and evaluate the inevitable emergence of socialism, as well as the tasks and solutions that need to be implemented in the process of building socialism in general and in Vietnam in particular.
- G5.Apply the scientific viewpoints of Marxism-Leninism to identify the schemes of peaceful evolution by reactionary and hostile forces.
- G6.Assess the scientific and revolutionary nature of Marxism-Leninism in general and scientific socialism in particular."
- G7.Analyze the goals, ideals of socialism, and the path to socialism.
- G8.Analyze and evaluate the success of the renewal process initiated and led by the Communist Party of Vietnam.
