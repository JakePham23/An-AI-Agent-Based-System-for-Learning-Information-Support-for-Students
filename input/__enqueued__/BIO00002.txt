# COURSE: Sinh đại cương 2 (BIO00002)

## GENERAL INFORMATION
- **Course ID**: BIO00002
- **English Name**: Biology 2
- **Vietnamese Name**: Sinh đại cương 2

- **Semester**: (s) in which the module is taught: 3
- **Workload**: (incl. contact hours, self-study hours): Hours for lecture: ~ 37.5 hours Hours for laboratory session: ~ 0 hours Hours for self-study, examination preparation, other activities: ~ 86.25 hours

## COURSE DESCRIPTION
• COURSE TOPICS:
- Overview of plant morphology and nutrition
- Transport in vascular plants
- Mineral nutrition in plants
- Reproduction in seed plants
- Plant responses to internal and external signals
- Hormones and the endocrine system
- The nervous system
- Nutrition in animals
- The circulatory system and gas exchange
- The immune system
- Reproduction and embryonic development in animals
- Osmoregulation and the mechanisms of sensation and movement
- Introduction to Ecology
- Population and community ecology
- Ecosystems, the biosphere, and biodiversity conservation

## LEARNING OBJECTIVES (CLOs)
- G1.Acquire basic knowledge about plant morphology.
- G2.Understand the basic functional processes of plants in the life cycle.
- G3.Acquire basic knowledge about animal morphology.
- G4.Understand the basic functional processes of animals in the life cycle.
- G5.Understand the fundamental concepts of ecology.
- G6.Understand the significance of biodiversity and the role of biodiversity conservation for Vietnam and the world.
